Who We Are

Allyson Corporate Insurance was founded on the belief that insurance should be simple, accessible, and personal. We are a proudly Kenyan company offering innovative insurance solutions tailored to individuals, families, and businesses. Every client is a relationship, not a number.

With a dedicated team of experienced professionals, we are committed to delivering world-class customer service and comprehensive coverage plans designed to fit your unique needs.
